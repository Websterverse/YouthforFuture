Youth4Future Hackathon

Welcome to our 6-day Hybrid Hackathon, an exciting event dedicated to fostering innovation and sustainability! This hackathon brings together brilliant minds from diverse backgrounds to collaborate, create, and compete in a dynamic and inspiring environment.

Get Started Register

Event Structure:

The hackathon is structured into three main parts:

  • Registration: Form Filling & PPT Submission

    • Registration Period: From now until 15th August
    • Fee: No registration fee required
    • Platforms: Devfolio (link) and Google Form (link)
    • PPT Submission Window: 16th August to 17th August
    • Shortlisting: Judges will review and shortlist the
      submitted PPTs on 18th August. Only shortlisted teams will proceed to the online round.

  • Round 1: Online Round

    • Duration: 19th August to 23rd August
    • Proctoring: Continuous monitoring via Google Meet

  • Round 2: Offline Round

    • Date: 24th August
    • Venue: Will be notified later
    • Activities:
      • PPT Presentation: Each team will have a maximum of
        5 minutes to present their PPT.
      • Project Presentation: Teams will provide a detailed
        demonstration of their projects.
    • Result Announcement: 24th August

Rules:

  • The hackathon is open to students of any institution in their 1st/2nd/3rd/4th year enrolled in either BCA or B.Tech Degree programs.

  • Teams may consist of 2–4 participants.

  • Teams must ensure that they have filled out a Google Form along with Devfolio registration.

  • The presentation to be submitted must comply with a PPT template, which will be provided later.

  • Forking of GitHub repositories is not allowed.

  • Single commits in GitHub repositories are not allowed.

  • Modifications to the project repository outside of hackathon hours are not allowed.

  • For the offline round, bring your laptop, charger, and any other necessary devices or materials.

  • Participants are allowed to modify their PPT to present it in the offline round.

THEMES

Sustainable Development

As we face global challenges such as climate change, resource depletion, and environmental degradation, the need for sustainable solutions has never been more critical. This theme invites participants to develop innovative technologies, products, and strategies that promote environmental stewardship, social equity, and economic viability.

Open Innovation

Embracing the power of collective creativity, this theme encourages participants to think outside the box and leverage diverse perspectives to solve complex problems. Open Innovation is about breaking down barriers, fostering collaboration, and driving progress through shared knowledge and resources.
